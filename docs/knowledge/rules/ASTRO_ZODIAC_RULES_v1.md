## 1. Purpose

This document defines curated Darrow Code interpretation rules for the twelve Western zodiac signs. It is derived from approved source packs in original Darrow Code language. No content is copied from external sources.

---

## 2. Core framing rule

```text
A zodiac sign is a symbolic mode — an operating tone, not a personality label.

Sun sign = identity tone / authorship quality.
Moon sign = emotional rhythm / inner safety pattern.
Ascendant = social interface / first impression (only if birth time reliable).
Stellium in a sign = amplified operating mode.
Dominant sign by planet count = secondary tonal emphasis.

No sign alone defines the person.
Never open customer prose with "You are Aries…" — translate into lived pattern first.
No sign determines destiny, career outcome, health, or relationship result.
```

---

## 3. Sign-vs-constellation policy

Western zodiac signs are twelve equal 30-degree divisions of the ecliptic, not the physical constellations. They do not map one-to-one with astronomical sky regions.

```text
Use "sign" for the zodiac symbolic division.
Use "constellation" only for astronomical or mythic background.
Do not add Ophiuchus to Darrow CORE zodiac logic.
Do not tell users that NASA or astronomical research "changed their sign."
```

## 5. Sign groupings

### 5.1 By element

| Element | Signs | Tonal cluster |
|---|---|---|
| Fire | Aries, Leo, Sagittarius | initiative, warmth, visibility, direction |
| Earth | Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn | stabilization, form, material competence, patience |
| Air | Gemini, Libra, Aquarius | conceptualization, connection, distance, perspective |
| Water | Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces | emotional depth, memory, empathy, permeability |

### 5.2 By modality

| Modality | Signs | Tonal cluster |
|---|---|---|
| Cardinal | Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn | initiation, direction, launching |
| Fixed | Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius | consolidation, persistence, depth |
| Mutable | Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces | adaptation, transition, flexibility |

### 5.3 By polarity

| Polarity | Signs | Symbolic framing |
|---|---|---|
| Yang / active | Fire + Air signs | outward expression, projection, engagement |
| Yin / receptive | Earth + Water signs | inward processing, containment, absorption |

Use polarity as symbolic framing only — not gender or strength claims.

## 6. Multi-sign emphasis

When multiple planets occupy one sign, that sign's theme is amplified.

```text
Stellium rule: 3+ planets in a sign signals a concentrated operating mode.
The sign is not reduced to a single fixed trait — the theme is intensified and
appears in multiple functional areas simultaneously.
```

Absent elements in a chart often represent shadow or compensatory territory rather than absence of experience.

---

## 7. Global forbidden claims (all signs)

The following must not appear in any Darrow output derived from zodiac sign rules:

```text
lucky number / lucky color / lucky stone for a sign
best match / worst match compatibility
personality type claims based on sign alone
deterministic fate or career claims
health diagnoses from sign
"You are [sign]" as a character verdict
"NASA changed your sign"
Ophiuchus as a 13th CORE sign
astrology is scientifically proven
```

## 1. Purpose

This document defines safe Darrow Code use-case categories for future PLACE add-on output. Each bucket describes a goal context, the symbolic Darrow place meaning for that goal, the kind of environment archetype that tends to resonate, and illustrative city/region examples.

**Critical rule on examples:**

```text
All city/region examples in this document are ILLUSTRATIVE ONLY.
They represent archetypes — the kind of environment that fits the use case.
They are NOT personalized recommendations.
They are NOT rankings.
They are NOT advice to move, visit, or orient toward any specific location.

Required wording: "places like...", "as an archetype...", "this kind of environment...",
"for illustration...", "cities such as..."
```

## 2. Use-case buckets

### 2.1 Work / Career

```
purpose:              phases where professional output, ambition, and career development are the primary goal
darrow_place_meaning: environments that activate structure, visibility, competitive drive, and professional field access
place_archetype:      structured business and financial capitals; cities with strong professional infrastructure and international connectivity
safe_wording:         "environments with strong professional density and structured ambition fields"
illustrative_examples:
  - London, Frankfurt, Zurich, Singapore, New York, Warsaw, Dubai
  - "as archetypes of structured career-oriented environments with high professional field density"
forbidden_claims:
  - "this city guarantees career success"
  - "move here for your career"
  - "you will be promoted / hired / successful in X"
```

### 2.6 Spiritual / Retreat

```
purpose:              phases where inward orientation, contemplation, spiritual practice, and withdrawal from ordinary stimulation are primary
darrow_place_meaning: environments with a cultural and geographic orientation toward the sacred, the contemplative, or the natural sublime
place_archetype:      places with historical or geographic depth that naturally quieten the ordinary mind — mountain regions, ancient towns, monastery contexts, natural retreats
safe_wording:         "environments where the cultural and geographic depth naturally supports inward orientation and contemplative experience"
illustrative_examples:
  - Himalaya foothills, Rishikesh, Kyoto temple district, Sedona, Bali/Ubud, monastery towns of any tradition
  - "as archetypes of contemplative and spiritually oriented environments"
note:                 "Any religious or cultural site mentioned as an example must be treated with respect for the tradition it represents; use as symbolic archetype only"
forbidden_claims:
  - "spiritual awakening guaranteed in X"
  - "enlightenment comes from this place"
  - "you must go to X for your spiritual path"
  - "Mount Athos is accessible to everyone" — it is a cultural reference only; access is restricted
```

### 2.7 Long-term Home Base

```
purpose:              phases where stable long-term living, safety, quality of life, and sustainable day-to-day environments are the primary orientation
darrow_place_meaning: environments that combine safety, functional infrastructure, cultural richness, and a human scale that supports long-term well-being
place_archetype:      livable mid-size or mid-large cities known for safety, infrastructure quality, cultural life, and sustainable lifestyle pace
safe_wording:         "environments known for livability, functional infrastructure, and a balance of cultural richness and day-to-day sustainability"
illustrative_examples:
  - Vienna, Bratislava, Ljubljana, Munich, Copenhagen, Prague, Porto, safe mid-size cities with strong infrastructure
  - "as archetypes of stable, livable, well-functioning home-base environments"
forbidden_claims:
  - "this is your ideal home city"
  - "you must live in X"
  - "this city is perfect for you"
  - legal / visa / residency advice
```

### 2.8 Short-term Travel Reset

```
darrow_place_meaning: environments where the change of context itself is the primary value — the interruption of ordinary life patterns
place_archetype:      varied by chart pattern — may be island, coastal, mountain, historical, or culturally unfamiliar environments; the key quality is meaningful difference from the home environment
safe_wording:         "environments that provide a meaningful shift in context — a change sufficient to interrupt the usual operating pattern"
illustrative_examples:
  - islands, coastal towns, lake regions, mountain resorts, spa towns, old historical cities
  - "as archetypes of reset-oriented short-term travel environments"
forbidden_claims:
  - "this trip will solve your problems"
  - "travel to X will change your life"
  - health or emotional outcome guarantees from travel
```

### 2.11 Body / Health-Rhythm Support

```
darrow_place_meaning: environments that support the body's natural rhythm through clean air, walkability, outdoor access, thermal resources, and lower stress density
```

### 2.12 Reinvention / New Chapter

```
purpose:              phases where conscious departure from prior patterns, reinvention of identity, and the beginning of a significantly different life chapter are primary
darrow_place_meaning: environments that carry enough cultural novelty, openness, and practical flexibility to support genuine reinvention without requiring an established prior identity there
place_archetype:      cities with an established culture of arrival, reinvention, and openness to new residents building new lives; often digital-nomad or creative migration hubs
safe_wording:         "environments known for openness to new arrivals, cultural novelty, and the structural flexibility that supports building something genuinely new"
illustrative_examples:
  - Berlin, Lisbon, Tallinn, Tbilisi, Barcelona, Mexico City, Chiang Mai, digital-nomad hubs
  - "as archetypes of reinvention-oriented, open-to-arrival environments"
forbidden_claims:
  - "this city will reinvent you"
  - "new life guaranteed in X"
  - "leave everything and move to X"
  - "this place is your new beginning"
```

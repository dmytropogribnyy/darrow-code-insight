## 4. Module copy recommendations

### 4.1 CORE Report

**Current / approved concept:**
> "Your private birth code: how you think, react, choose and move through change."

**Status:** foundation product — approved concept, do not change without explicit review.

**Supporting copy options:**
> "The symbolic blueprint beneath your decisions."
> "Not your star sign. Your full pattern."
> "A reading built from your actual chart — not a horoscope."

**What CORE covers:**
- Identity architecture and operating mode
- Emotional rhythm and inner safety pattern
- Shadow patterns and productive friction
- Relational tendencies (personal pattern)
- Professional archetype
- Environmental resonance
- Numerology code (Life Path, Birthday)
- Executive synthesis

**Guard:** CORE includes only soft environmental archetype (1–3 illustrative place examples if chart strongly supports it); no city ranking, no relocation advice, no maps, no astrocartography.

## 5. Copy tone rules

All product copy must follow Darrow voice:

```text
✅ Recognition-first: the reader recognizes their own pattern
✅ Warm and precise: not clinical, not vague
✅ Non-deterministic: no fate, destiny, or certainty claims
✅ Specific enough to intrigue, open enough to apply broadly
✅ Not astrology jargon for the non-astrologer
```

```text
❌ "Your lucky [anything]"
❌ "Guaranteed to [outcome]"
❌ "Your destiny is..."
❌ "Your chart says you must..."
❌ "Perfect for [sun sign] people"
❌ Sun-sign reduction in any copy
❌ "The world's best astrology" / superlative claims
```
